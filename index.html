History of Durga Puja in Chudangpur

The Durga Puja in Chudangpur dates back several decades, starting as a small gathering of villagers. Over the years, the celebration has transformed into a large community event, drawing visitors from nearby areas. It reflects the cultural diversity of the village, blending traditional rituals with modern festivities. Each year, the community comes together to design beautiful pandals and showcase cultural programs, preserving the heritage for future generations.

Upcoming Plans for Durga Puja 2024

  • *Live Streaming:* Experience puja ceremonies and events through live streaming for devotees worldwide.
  • *Virtual Pandal Tour:* A 360-degree immersive tour of the main pandal for those unable to attend in person.
  • *Eco-Friendly Practices:* Use of biodegradable materials for idols and decorations, promoting sustainability.
  • *Cultural Events:* Competitions such as dance, music, and rangoli-making to engage the youth.
  • *Procession and Immersion:* Grand procession with traditional music and eco-friendly immersion in the village pond.
